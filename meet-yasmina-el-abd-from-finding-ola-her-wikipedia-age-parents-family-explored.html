Meet Yasmina El-Abd From Finding Ola; Her Wikipedia Age; Parents &amp; Family Explored

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, June 8, 2024

15-years-old American/Egyptian/Swiss actor and singer Yasmina El-Abd rose to fame from the incredible performance ability she showed in the new Netflix original series, Finding Ola.

Yasmina El-Abd started her professional acting career at a very young age. At 9, she stepped into professional acting and started her singing career at12.

The actress starred in many commercials, tourism campaigns, music videos, musicals, and short movies. She presented the TV show for National Geographic Kids Abu Dhabi.

The movie The Shadow of Cairo, where she won the audience’s hearts, got nominated for the Excellence Award in Smart Screen Creative Awards of MET Film School in London in 2018.

The movie also got selected in 2019 for the Jury price for the best short-action film of NYICFF (New York International Children’s Film festival- Oscar-qualifying festival) and ECU (European Independent Film Festival).

Yasmina El-Abd’s Age: How Old Is She?

Yasmina El-Abd was born in Switzerland on 8 June 2006. She is now 15-years-old.

She spent her entire childhood in Switzerland and started her acting career at 9.

The actress is very passionate about acting, singing, and dancing. She wanted to be an actress from her childhood, and to fulfill her dream, she has been a part of many workshops and training.

She attended Auditionning technique training in Intisar Issa Dubai and did her acting course from Marwa Gabriel Starz Cair. She also participated in Musical Theatre workshop in Diverse performing Arts school Dubai and learned drama techniques from TRINITY London.

The actress is a trained singer and got solo singing training from LAMDA London.
