Boris Kodjoe's Wife Nicole Ari Parker Defies Her Age Rocking a Mesh Hooded Top with a Headband

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, July 23, 2024

Nicole Ari Parker recently showed off her timeless beauty in a new Instagram photo and fans could barely stop gushing over the actress' striking looks.

Boris Kodjoe's wife Nicole Ari Parker might be in her fifties already, but once again, the actress has proven that she is a timeless beauty with a stunning share on Instagram.

Parker posted a photo of herself donning a black sheer hooded top pulled over a black headband. With a few of her ginger locks falling across her face, the actress looked super chic.

Actress Nicole Ari Parker attends the 2019 "Go Red For Women's Dress Collection" of The American Heart Society in New York City. | Photo: Getty Images

Parker sported perfectly manicured fingers coated in white nail polish. Posing for the photo, she flashed a charming smile that showed off her dimples. Captioning the post, the actress wrote:

"When walking the dog & carrying in the groceries = a workout????‍♀️ ?."

As expected, Parker's post stirred up several reactions from fans and followers who took to the comments section gushing about the actress's good looks.

While one user commented that Parker looked fabulous, another complimented the actress on her beauty. Many others simply left heart and fire emojis in admiration.

Over the years, Parker has proved herself to be a beauty and fitness enthusiast. Unsurprisingly, the actress has flawless skin and a fit body to show for it.

Back in 2014, Parker opened up about her beauty secret, revealing that not only does she use coconut oil on her skin, but she also ingests the natural oil.

On how she maintains her fit physique, Parker revealed to Us Magazine that although she does not frequent the gym, it does not stop her from having a fitness plan.

Apart from her love for self-care, Parker also has a great love for her husband Boris Kidjoe and their two teenage kids, daughter Sophie and son Nicolas.

Parker met Kodjoe when they were cast as on-screen lovers in the classic series "Soul Food" and soon fell in love. After dating for a while, the pair got married, and ever since, it has been nothing but bliss.

Speaking about the secret to their long-lasting love, Parker revealed that it is necessary to always take care of oneself. According to her, this is important in maintaining a relationship.

Going by how Kodjoe spoils his wife, there is no doubt that the couple is still very much in love with each other even after years of being together.

To celebrate their 15th wedding anniversary last year, Kodjoe organized a surprise private beachside celebration that left Parker and many of their fans gushing.

With a happy marriage, glowing skin, and an amazing body, Parker is evidently living her best life. Even at 50, the actress remains as breathtaking as ever!
