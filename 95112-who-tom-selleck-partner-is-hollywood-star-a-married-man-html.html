Who is Tom Selleck partner? Is the Hollywood star a married man?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, May 2, 2024

Tom Selleck is a renowned Hollywood actor best known for playing the role of investigator Thomas Magnum in Magnum P.I., an 80s hit TV series, and of Commissioner Frank Reagan in the Blue Bloods series. Tom Selleck partner is actress Jillie Mack. The couple's marriage is one of the most successful in Hollywood as they have been together since 1987.

In the early 1990s, there were speculations that he might be gay, but he refuted the claims saying he is heterosexual. Who is Tom Selleck wife, and what is the secret to his successful marriage? Find out below.

Is Tom Selleck married?

Does Tom Selleck have a wife? The veteran Hollywood actor is a happily married man. His partner is Jillie Joan Mack, an actress from London. The couple tied the knot in 1987 and welcomed their only daughter Hannah Margaret in 1988.

The award-winning actor chose to keep his marriage life with Mack private and to make it a priority. This worked because the couple has been married for more than three decades, contrary to Hollywood beliefs that celebrity marriages end as soon as they start.

The star made this crucial decision because fame and constant spotlight destroyed his first marriage with the model Jacqueline Ray. Tom and Jacqueline got married in 1971 and adopted their son Kevin together. The union did not last long as they went their separate ways after divorcing in 1982.

The Magnum PI actor is currently leading a humble life with his wife Jillie Mack and daughter Hannah at his Ventura ranch in California. He is concentrating on cultivating avocado trees.

Who is Tom Selleck wife, Jillie Mack?

Jillie Mack is popularly recognized for being Tom Selleck's wife. She was born on 25th December 1957 in Wiltshire, England. She started developing a love for dancing and acting from a young age.

She had a breakthrough with her role as Rumpleteazer in the successful Cats musical. After moving to the USA, she has appeared in several movies and series, including Magnum PI as Sally, Silverfox as Joanie, Frasier, and Police Story.

The actress was a 25-year-old dancer for the Cats musical when she met the then 38-year-old Tom, a divorcee who was filming on location in England in 1983. After her contract with the Cats concluded, she left London for Hawaii to be with Tom. They later had a secret wedding in 1987 and have been together ever since.

Is Tom Selleck gay?

In 1991, there were speculations that the actor is homosexual after unknown people in New York City placed a placard with his image and a statement saying, Absolutely Queer. Globe tabloid used the information to publish an article called Gay Stars Stop Traffic.

Selleck filed a $20 million libel and invasion of privacy lawsuit against the tabloid. He accused them of republishing untrue remarks that falsely portray him as being gay. The tabloid later published an apology after Tom won the lawsuit.

Tom Selleck net worth

According to Celebrity Net Worth, the actor is worth approximately $45 million. He earns from acting. His recent project is the Blue Blood television series, where he plays the role of a police commissioner. He is said to make about $200 thousand from a single episode of the series.

Tom Selleck partner, Jillie Joan Mack, is a lucky woman. Her husband is dedicated to ensuring that their family stays together till the end. It is indeed possible for a Hollywood marriage to have a happy ending.
